# Inbox Test Emails (20 Samples)

These are ready-to-send sample emails for testing the inbox listener flow routing in `EmailIdleService`.

- Samples 1-10 target **POLICY** flow (Office Space-inspired scenarios)
- Samples 11-20 target **EMPLOYEE** flow (employee counts and employee-specific data)

---

**Body:**

I had a serious case of the Mondays and showed up in jeans and sneakers. What is Mega Corp's official policy on business casual vs formal attire, and are there exceptions on specific days?

Thanks,
